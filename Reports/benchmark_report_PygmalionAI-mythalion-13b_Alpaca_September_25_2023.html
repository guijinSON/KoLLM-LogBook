Response 101

Absurdism is a philosophical concept that challenges the fundamental purpose and meaning of human existence. It suggests that the world we live in is inherently absurd, lacking any objective purpose or meaning. The term "break the fourth wall" refers to a theatrical convention where a performer addresses the audience directly, acknowledging their presence and involvement in the performance.
